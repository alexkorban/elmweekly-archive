Issue #27

A round up of what’s happening in the wonderful world of the Elm programming language.

News

elm-lang.org Home Page Redesign

Evan Czaplicki brings clarity to the elm-lang home page.

Learn Elm, 2-Day Workshop

Richard Feldman is teaching a 2-day Elm course with Frontend-Masters. Attend online or in-person on 8-9 Sept.

Reading

Using Elm in the newsroom

Elliot Davies delivers a balanced verdict after using Elm in production for The Times. (site | code)

Blazing Fast HTML: Round 2

Evan Czaplicki shares the results of his virtual-dom rewrite with a sound presentation of Elm’s benefits over other popular frameworks.  

The Elm Architecture

Ralf Westphal shows us what the Elm Architecture looks like in this gradual unveiling.

Loops to Folds

Folkert de Vries translates common imperative loop patterns to Elm.

Decoding Large JSON Objects

Brian Hicks tackles decoding large JSON objects with a breakdown of three approaches.

Rob Hoelz takes a walk through a partially ordered forest.

This question on the mailing list features answers from Richard Feldman and Soren Debois.

Server side Elm with Phoenix

An experiment with Server side Elm and Phoenix conducted by Daniel Neighman.

Kyle J. Kress demonstrates that Elm has the tools for i18n at the language level in Translations Using Unions in Elm.

10 reasons why you should give Elm a try — Medium

Thibaut Assus convinces us to try Elm. (Petr Huřťák tempers the enthusiasm but fails to dissuade us)

Sacha Greif touches on Elm in his review of the JavaScript landscape.

Watching & Listening

TDDing a URL encoding/decoding library

Building a basic web app and Mandelbrot Magic in these 4 Elm live streaming sessions from Aaron VonderHaar.

elm-test Reading Session

Read The Source features Richard Feldman talking through elm-test.
