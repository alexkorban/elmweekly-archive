Issue #30

A round up of what’s happening in the wonderful world of the Elm programming language.

News

Elm Slack Archive

Thanks to Luke Westby and SlackArchive.io there is now a public chat archive for the popular Elm Slack.

Introduction to Elm Workshop

Richard Feldman will be giving a workshop on Elm as part of Reactive Conf in Bratislava, Slovakia on October 26th. 

Reading

Web Frameworks, Proof of Concept, and Building an Inbox Simulation

This story from Adam Stewart really sells the benefits of Elm in a client setting.

Giles Bowkett: Elm Is The New Rails

Giles Bowkett writes about having fun when programming and why Elm fits the bill.

Does Elm Harmonize With F#?

An enjoyable account of using Elm and F# together from Lucas Reis.

Building Reactive WordPress Plugins – Elm

Ian Jones builds a reactive WordPress plugin using Elm and compares it to previous efforts in Backbone.js and Vue.js.

Two Talking Maybes is Maybe Too Many

Brian Hicks extracts some wisdom from Richard Feldman’s Elm Conf talk, Making Impossible States Impossible.

Software That Writes And Evolves Software

Rod Johnson of Atomist, explains the tool for helping you write software that Jessica Kerr showed off at Elm Conf last week.

Adding Elm to a Rails application

Abe Voelker gives a nice run-through of integrating Elm into your Rails application with Webpack.

Functions + State Management > Components

Damien Klinnert looks at Elm vs. React from the point-of-view of someone new to both.

Why Elm is Going to Change the World

Josh Adams shares why he thinks Elm is a world changer.

The Return Monad

Isaac Shapira wants us to harness the power of (model, Cmd msg).

Programming: Elm vs ClojureScript, a first encounter

Timothy Pratley compares Elm and ClojureScript.

Applying User-Centered Design to Errors

Damien Klinnert shares how Elm (and Haskell) has influenced the way he builds software. Also Functions + State Management > Components.

Watching & Listening

Pairing Session: Josh Adams and Corey Haines

In this pairing session from DailyDrip, Josh Adams and Corey Haines work through building a parser (driven by tests) for Game of Life.

Collaborative Music with Elm and Phoenix

Josh Adam’s talk from Elixir Conf at the beginning of the month.

Editing In Elm Town Episode 1

The first episode of a new podcast about Elm and its people from Murphy Randle with Jessica Kerr and Matthew Griffith.

Code

Elm Repl

elm-repl in your browser from Girish Sonawane.

elm-ast 1.0.0

Bogdan Popa has written a parser for Elm in Elm.

elm-date-selector 1.0.0

Justin Mimbs has put together a DateSelector package.

ChaeTree

An Elm implementation of a RoseTree related data structure by Marek Fajkus.

Source code for Pairs.one

The multiplayer Phoenix and Elm game from last week now has source code available, by Max Gorin.
