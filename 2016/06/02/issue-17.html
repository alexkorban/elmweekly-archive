News

elm-conf, the conference for Elm, has issued an open call for papers and confirmed Evan Czaplicki as keynote speaker. Find out more on the elm-conf Website. 

To go, you need tickets for the StrangeLoop Conference. Registration opens this Wednesday, June 8th, 2016 at 12pm US Central Time (CST) and tickets are expected to sell out within the first or second hour.
