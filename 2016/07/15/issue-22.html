Issue #22

A round up of what’s happening in the wonderful world of the Elm programming language.

News

How to Use Elm at Work

Evan Czaplicki outlines his advised approach to adopting Elm in this official blog post. 

elm-conf 2016 Schedule

The elm-conf schedule has been announced, featuring an array (list?) of interesting speakers and talks. The conference takes place on September 15th in St Louis, Missouri with tickets still available.

Reading

Duplicating Scientists in Elm: Stop Sharing State

Brian Hicks shows us how to prevent bugs by avoiding unnecessary duplication.

Writing A Full Site in Phoenix and Elm

Brandon Richey continues his series with a focus on views and navigation.

Undo and redo in Elm - macwright.org

Tom MacWright implements undo and redo in Elm as a follow-on from his similar takes on Immutable.js and ClojureScript.

Elm and Event Sourcing

An interesting article from Marco Perone looking at using event sourcing in your Elm application.

After React: Elm?

Freeman Latif persuades React developers to make the jump to Elm.

SPAs in Elm: Part Two - Functional Routing

Kevin Greene continues his tutorial with a look at navigation for a Single Page Application.

A fun first app with Elm

This beginner article goes one step further than the official guide, integrating web sockets and a UI library. 

Watching & Listening

My adventure with Elm

Yan Cui’s gentle introduction to Elm featuring the game Snake, delivered two weeks ago at PolyConf 16.

Elm for JavaScript Developers

Jack Franklin introduces Elm to a JavaScript audience at PolyConf 16.

Introduction to Elm 0.17

Claudia Doppioslash gave an introduction to Elm 0.17 at a Cambridge DDD Nights.

Evan Czaplicki

The Elixir Fountain podcast had Elm creator Evan Czaplicki on as a guest in their latest episode.

Code

Elm Chat

A simple chat server written in Crystal (Kemal) with an Elm front end by Ryan L. Bell. (demo)

NDC Oslo 2016 Videos

A viewer for the NDC Oslo 2016 videos written in F# and Elm by Håkon Rossebø.

Deep Finesse

William Bailey’s Bridge hand analyser written in Elm with support for touch.

Tac Tac Toe

A simple game of Tac Tac Toe coded up in Elm by Zach Green.
