Issue #21

A round up of what’s happening in the wonderful world of the Elm programming language.

News

elm news

Jacob Oakes has written a news aggregator for Elm in Elm, pulling in data from elm-discuss, elm-dev, hacker news, and reddit.

Reading

Writing Friendly Elm Code

Tessa Kelly offers some great advice on keeping your Elm code friendly rather than fancy, illustrated with code.

Duplicate, Message, or Update? Contexts in Elm Components

Brian Hicks argues for the best of three options when sharing context with components.

Pipes, Chaining and Nesting

Claudia Doppioslash resumes her Road To Elm series with an exploration of the options for function composition. 

A few notes on Elm 0.17 - Composing Applications

Rob Ashton's discusses application composition in Elm in his Notes on Elm.

Getting Started with a New Language

Part one of a four part series on Single-Page Web Apps in Elm by Kevin Greene.

Watching & Listening

What the heck is Elm?

Full Stack Radio host Adam Wathan talks to Joel Clermont about the Elm programming language and getting started with functional programming.

Content Catalog, Part 3: Topics and Content

In this free screencast episode of Daily Drip, Josh Adams looks at Nested Routes and modularity.

Code

Paint the Town Red

This game from Michael Bylstra does what it says on the tin. I haven’t broken 20 seconds yet. (code)

Elmsweeper

The classic game of Minesweeper implemented in Elm by Greg Reimer.

Diamondback Railroad

A Board Game in Elm as a learning exercise and potentially a board game engine from Scott Smith.
